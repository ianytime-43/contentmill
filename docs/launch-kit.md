## 2. Product Hunt Tagline + Description

**Tagline** (54 chars)

```
Paste once, get 20+ platform-ready posts instantly
```

**Short Description** (253 chars)

```
Turn any blog post into 20+ ready-to-publish pieces for Twitter, LinkedIn, newsletters, and Instagram. AI adapts your voice and tone for each platform. What used to take hours now takes 30 seconds. Free to start.
```

**Full Description**

ContentMill is an AI-powered content repurposing tool. You paste in a blog post, newsletter, video script, or any long-form content, and it generates 20+ platform-specific posts ready to copy and publish.

The key difference from generic AI rewriting: ContentMill actually adapts your tone for each platform. Your Twitter output reads like native Twitter. Your LinkedIn post has the right professional framing. Your newsletter excerpt has its own hook. You are not just getting the same text slightly reworded five times.

How it works:
- Paste your source content
- Choose which platforms you want (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, newsletters, or all of them)
- Get platform-optimized posts in seconds

Built for creators, marketers, founders, and anyone who publishes across multiple channels and is tired of the reformatting grind.

Free tier includes 3 repurposes per month with access to all platforms. Pro ($29/mo) gives you 50 repurposes with priority processing. Unlimited ($79/mo) removes all limits and adds API access.
